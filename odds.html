Questions

FAQ

Are the odds truly random? ▼
Yes. Each slot uses a seeded random number generator against the published probability table. Guaranteed slots override the base roll — meaning if Slot 4 is guaranteed Silver or higher, the system rolls only within Silver, Gold, and Diamond at proportional rates.
Can I sell or trade items? ▼
When the marketplace feature launches, you will be able to list items from your inventory for other Looklist users to purchase. Order Home allows physical delivery of eligible items. Both features are in development.
Do keys expire? ▼
Earned keys (from completed bookings) never expire. Purchased keys also carry no expiry under current policy. Looklist reserves the right to introduce expiry on promotional keys only, with 30 days notice.
What is LLD balance? ▼
LLD (Looklist Digital) is the platform balance used within the Luck Keys store. It can be topped up via standard payment methods. Looklist Credits won inside boxes are added directly to your LLD balance.
What happens if I win a Diamond item? ▼
Diamond items are flagged in your inventory immediately. You can choose to order them home (physical delivery), hold them, or list on the marketplace. High-value items like wigs and hampers include a verification step before dispatch.
